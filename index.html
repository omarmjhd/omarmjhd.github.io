About Me

I'm a Product Manager focused on growth and user experience with previous experience in Software Engineering currently looking into opportunities in the Product Management space.

These days I'm working full time as a Product Manager at Indeed in Austin, Texas, managing and driving software projects to better connect job seekers and employers worldwide.

Previously at Indeed, I worked as a Research Engineer I and II, working to build software to improve Indeed's SEO performance, as well and focus on growth for the Job Seeker platform. I started my career as a Software Engineer I and II at Bazaarvoice in Austin, Texas, developing software to better help retailers understand what customers are saying about their products.

I graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology in December 2016 with a Bachelor's of Science Degree in Computer Science and a minor in Economics.

While in school, I was able to intern 4 separate times with Harris Corporation, Boeing, JPMorgan Chase & Co and VeriSolutions (an Atlanta based startup).

I've lived in Austin for just over 6 years, by way of Atlanta, after graduating from Georgia Tech. When not at work or working on interesting side projects, I enjoy going to a variety of different concerts, and playing golf.
