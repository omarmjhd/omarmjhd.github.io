About Me

I'm an experienced Software Engineer currently looking into opportunities in the Product Management space.

These days I'm working full time as a Research Engineer II at Indeed in Austin, Texas, managing and driving software projects to better connect job seekers and employers worldwide.

Previously, I worked as a Software Engineer I and II at Bazaarvoice in Austin, Texas, developing software to better help retailers understand what customers are saying about their products.

I graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology in December 2016 with a Bachelor's of Science Degree in Computer Science and a minor in Economics.

While in school, I was able to intern 4 separate times with Harris Corporation, Boeing, JPMorgan Chase & Co and VeriSolutions (an Atlanta based startup).

When not writing code and working on interesting side projects, I enjoy going to a variety of different concerts, and playing golf.
